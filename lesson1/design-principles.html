White Space and Clean Design Wealth Simple

This website is a clear example of how clean it looks with White Space around it. It enhances the user's readability experience and increases comprehension because there is only the necessary text, and it uses good padding between content and borders.

PARC: Alignment
Pinterest


Pinterest is an excellent alignment website example because you can feel the sense of direction, unity, and organization. It also creates a balance between the images and the small text it appears, making a connection. It is clean, neat, readable, and shows the audience's essential things to see.

P.A.R.C.: Contrast
Estudio Tahmek

Estudio Tahmek's website is an excellent example of how it has contrast. The background is black, and then the words are orange. This highlight the design and make it memorable for the viewers. This also helps lead the viewer's eye towards what is most important. It has a good balance and is very simple yet very elegant.
