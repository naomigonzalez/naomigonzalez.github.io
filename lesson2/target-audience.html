Target Audience

The target audience for this site is focused on people who are either

  • 1. Into fashion and always want to know what to wear
  • 2. People that just want to see if they need to carry an umbrella, a jacket or if it will be sunny for a short

    • Persona 1:

  • Malú Vargas
  • 27 years old
  • Status: Married
  • Mother of 2
  • Photographer
  • Owns a production studio

    • Persona 2:

  • Misael Iniestra
  • 22 years old
  • Status: Single
  • Applied Bussiness student
  • Works in a call center

    • Scenario 1:

    A couple wants to check the weather to know if they can have dates outdoors like hiking, riding bikes, or a romantic picnic. Or if they should have an indoor date like a museum, going to a mall or watching movies at home

    Scenario 2:

    Malú the owner of a production studio has to film a video outdoors, she wants to know when will be the best day and time to shoot it.

    Scenario 3:

    Olaf is someone who likes excersicing everyday. He uses this site to know what sport clothes should he wear.

    Scenario 4:

    Maya goes to school in the night shift, she wants to know the perfect outfit to wear at school

    Scenario 5:

    Veronica is a mother of three kids and takes them to extra curricular classes in the evening. She likes to be prepare and tells them if they need to wear a sweater or not
