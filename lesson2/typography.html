Typography

Bebas Neue

For the titles, I decided that we will use the typography of Bebas Neue font in its Regular 400 style.

Roboto

Fot the text in the paragraphs, I will use the Roboto font in its Light 300 style.

I chose these two fonts because they are clean fonts and easy to read, so the people who visit our site can have a good experience no matter on which device they are.
